Ashwagandha

Ashwagandha is a powerful adaptogenic herb known for reducing stress, improving stamina, and boosting immunity. Perfect for modern lifestyle challenges.
Ashwagandha (Withania Somnifera)
Ashwagandha, scientifically known as Withania Somnifera, is one of the most revered herbs in Ayurvedic medicine. This remarkable adaptogen has been used for over 3,000 years to enhance vitality and help the body manage stress.

The herb works by regulating cortisol levels, supporting adrenal function, and enhancing the body's natural ability to cope with physical and mental stressors. Studies show it can improve cognitive function, increase muscle mass and strength, and promote deeper, more restful sleep patterns.

Active Compound

Withanolides

Soil Type

Sandy loam, well-drained

Duration

150–180 days

Best For

Stress relief, stamina boost

Avoid If

Thyroid disorders, pregnancy

Tulsi

Tulsi, or Holy Basil, is known as the "Queen of Herbs" in Ayurveda. It supports respiratory health, boosts immunity, and has anti-inflammatory properties. Widely grown for both medicinal and spiritual purposes.
Tulsi (Holy Basil)
Tulsi, or Holy Basil, is known as the "Queen of Herbs" in Ayurveda. It supports respiratory health, boosts immunity, and has anti-inflammatory properties.

This sacred herb is widely grown for both medicinal and spiritual purposes, offering natural wellness benefits for daily health maintenance.

Active Compound

Eugenol, Ursolic acid

Soil Type

Loamy, well-drained

Duration

60–90 days

Best For

Respiratory health, immunity

Avoid If

On blood-thinning medication

Cardamom

Cardamom is a fragrant spice known for its sweet, slightly citrusy flavor and strong antioxidant properties. It supports digestion, freshens breath, and is used in both culinary and medicinal traditions.
Cardamom
Cardamom, often called the "Queen of Spices," is valued for its aromatic pods that enhance both sweet and savory dishes. It promotes digestive health, reduces inflammation, and has antimicrobial properties.

Traditionally used in Ayurveda and other systems of medicine, cardamom supports respiratory health and overall wellness.

Active Compound

Cineole, Terpinyl acetate

Soil Type

Well-drained loamy soil, rich in organic matter

Duration

2–3 years (first harvest)

Best For

Digestive health, respiratory wellness

Avoid If

Gallstones, severe acid reflux

Clove

Cloves are rich in antioxidants, help fight bacteria, reduce inflammation, support digestion, and may help regulate blood sugar.
Clove (Syzygium aromaticum)
Clove contains eugenol, which provides pain relief, fights microbial infections, and improves digestion. Traditionally used for toothaches, sore throats, bloating, and maintaining oral health. It also supports metabolic health and protects cells from oxidative damage.

Active Compound

Eugenol

Soil Type

Well-drained loamy soil

Duration

6–8 years (first harvest)

Best For

Oral health, digestion, pain relief

Avoid If

Bleeding disorders

Cinnamon

Cinnamon is a natural antioxidant, helps control blood sugar, supports heart health, reduces inflammation, and fights some microbes.
Cinnamon (Cinnamomum verum)
Containing cinnamaldehyde, cinnamon supports diabetes management, reduces cholesterol, and protects against oxidative stress. It’s used for digestive issues, infection prevention, and may offer neuroprotective benefits.

Active Compound

Cinnamaldehyde

Soil Type

Sandy loam, slightly acidic

Duration

2–3 years (first harvest)

Best For

Blood sugar control, digestion

Avoid If

Liver problems, pregnancy (large doses)

Turmeric

Turmeric has powerful anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects, supports liver and heart health, aids digestion, and may help protect against certain diseases.
Turmeric (Curcuma longa)
Curcumin, turmeric’s active compound, is known to reduce inflammation, improve arthritis symptoms, protect the liver, and boost brain function. It may help prevent chronic diseases and support overall wellness.

Active Compound

Curcumin

Soil Type

Well-drained, fertile loamy soil

Duration

7–9 months

Best For

Arthritis relief, digestion, liver support

Avoid If

Gallbladder disease, surgery soon
